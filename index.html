About Martin

"A retired businessman with a lifelong fascination for motor racing, local history, and the stories that define Britain."

Martin Rowley brings decades of experience and an authentic passion for storytelling to every speaking engagement. Having worked closely with legends like Sir Stirling Moss and developed deep knowledge of the British motor industry, Martin's talks are both informative and deeply entertaining.

His unique blend of wit, intelligence, and warmth keeps audiences on the edge of their seats while delivering valuable insights into motorsport, crime and policing, social history, and the UK justice system. Martin's nostalgic yet forward-looking perspective makes every talk a memorable experience.
